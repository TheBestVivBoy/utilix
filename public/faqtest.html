Frequently Asked Questions

Is Utilix free?

Yes, Utilix is completely free to use and is intended to remain free in the future.

Is there a web dashboard?

Yes, Utilix operates within Discord and a web dashboard.

How do I report a scammer?

Use the /report command and follow the prompts. Always provide a clear reason — reports without valid reasons will be declined.

How long are scammer reports stored?

Scammer reports are stored permanently as part of the bot’s core functionality.

How do I configure the bot for my server?

Use the /setup command to begin configuring Utilix for your server’s needs or go to the website's dashboard.

Who can use the bot?

Anyone with the proper Discord permissions on a server can invite and use Utilix. Some features may be restricted to server admins or staff. (Note: this privilege can be taken away from those who break our Terms of Service.)

Can users view scammer reports?

Yes! Anyone can use the /check command to look up users in the scammer report database.

Can I remove a scammer report?

No. Reports are permanent and can only be removed or modified by the official support team if needed.

Who manages scam reports and how can I view them?

Scam reports are reviewed and controlled only by the two official support members: thebestvivboy and infinity_for_life_. You can use the /check command to view reported users, but only they can manage the database.

Introduction & Purpose

This handbook exists to keep Ethan's World staff consistent, fair, and professional while retaining the playful community energy our server is known for. It covers commands, escalation paths, documentation, and activity expectations ‚Äî follow it, ask for clarification when needed, and lean on higher-ranked staff when unsure.

Staff Guidelines

  • Respect & Professionalism: Treat all members and staff with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or misconduct‚Äîinside or outside the community‚Äîwill not be tolerated.
  • Impartial Moderation: Handle all situations fairly. Personal conflicts must never influence your decisions.
  • Lead by Example: Follow server rules and uphold high standards of behavior. You represent the server‚Äîyour actions set the tone.
  • Behavior Outside the Server: Staff should maintain a positive reputation outside the community.
  • Confidentiality: Keep staff discussions, reports, and plans private.
  • Proper Report & Punishment Handling: Investigate carefully before taking action and document all significant actions.
  • Active Participation & Communication: Meet weekly staff requirements and communicate absences.
  • No Invisible Mode: Staff must remain visible for accountability and prompt response.
  • Role & Promotion Rules: Do not assign roles to yourself or others without permission. Promotions are earned through performance.
  • Seek Guidance: If unsure about a rule or decision, contact higher-ranked staff.

Commands (by role)

Commands below are role-gated. Use them responsibly ‚Äî misuse causes staff warnings.

Trainee Moderators

!warn [user] [reason]
!mute [user] [duration] [reason]

Trainee Mods should consult a full Moderator before executing non-obvious actions; training is ongoing.

Moderators & Up

!kick [user] [reason]
!ban [user] [reason]

Moderators may execute kicks and bans when rules are broken and documented. For edge cases or appealable content, ping a Head Mod/Admin.

Misuse: Targeting members unfairly, using commands without documented reason, or applying actions to yourself will result in a staff warning.

üîá Mute & Warning Guidelines

Standard Procedure:

  • Verbal warning: Start with a verbal warning for minor offenses (spam, small rule-breaking, etc.).
  • 1st warning: Issue a warn (modstats entry).
  • 2 warnings within 1 hour: Mute for 1 hour.

Notes: Always include a clear reason when you issue a warn or mute and link the rule broken. Keep tone professional in public mod messages; be explicit and factual in logs.

Staff Guidelines & Rules

  • Respect & Professionalism — Treat members and staff with respect. (Even when they spam emoji soup.)
  • Impartial Moderation — Be fair and consistent. No favorites. Yes, even for the person who brings virtual donuts.
  • Lead by Example — You are the adult in the room. Or the slightly more responsible teen.
  • Behavior Outside — Your reputation elsewhere reflects on Ethan’s World. That includes your cactus-themed blog.
  • Confidentiality — Keep staff discussions private. Not even the parrots.
  • Report Handling — Investigate then act; log to modstats. Writing dramatic summaries is optional but encouraged.
  • Active Participation — Meet weekly requirements or use LOA. Ghosting is reserved for supernatural beings only.
  • No Invisible Mode — Stay visible for accountability. This is not a stealth game.
  • Role & Promotion Rules — No self-assigning roles. Asking politely while juggling is still asking.
  • Seek Guidance — Ask higher staff when unsure. Interpretive dance not required.
